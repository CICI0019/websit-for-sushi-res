Menu

Salmon Nigiri - (85 calories) a slice of raw salmon served over pressed vinegar rice.
Kappa Maki - (120 calories) a simple sushi roll (maki) made with cucumber encased in sushi rice and dried seaweed.
Tekka maki - (134 calories) a cylindrical sushi that sushi rice wrapped in a nori (dried laver)

Kanpyo Maki - (173 calories) a sushi rolled with sweet-simmered kanpyo (dried gourd strip)
California roll - (230 calories) an inside-out “makizushi” roll with cucumber, crab meat (or imitation crab meat), and avocado with white rice.
Spicy tuna Roll - (290 calories) sushi rice, nori, and sashimi-grade tuna topped with sesame seeds and green onions/scallions.

Dynamite Roll - (310 calories) a piece of prawn tempura and masago, with vegetables, avocado and cucumber, as well as Japanese mayonnaise.
Spider Roll Soft Shell Crab - (400 calories) filled with soft-shell crab tempura, cucumber, and carrots, then rolled in nori seaweed sheet and sushi rice.
Shrimp Tempura Roll - (508 calories) baked and battered shrimp, sushi rice, nori, cucumber, and avocado.
